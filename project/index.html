What is this?

Legend Engine is a narrative-focused, genre agnostic Table RPG system developed by Taloir. While Legend Engine can provide a sophisticated tactical combat experience unrivaled by popular TRPGs, it's main design focus is in guiding players to write dynamic, compelling and well paced narratives on the fly. You'll also be equipped to explore themes that matter to you and challenge your characters' beliefs: as well as your own, if you're comfortable getting them involved.

Titans Wager

Titans Wager, the default setting of Legend Engine, is a greek-western hybrid of mythological proportions! In a world of monsters, gods, lawless settlements and wild frontiers, your characters will be worldshaking heroes the likes of Hercules of Greek mythology, Davy Crockett of American folklore, and Coyote of American Indian mythology.
